Welcome to our comprehensive guide on postpartum depression (PPD). At Motherly, we understand that becoming a new mother is a significant life transition filled with joy, excitement, and challenges. However, for some women, this period can also bring about feelings of sadness, anxiety, and overwhelming stress. Postpartum depression is a common condition that affects many new mothers, but with the right support and information, it can be managed effectively. It typically involves several stages and should only be diagnosed by specified tests as prescribed.
